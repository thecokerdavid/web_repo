About Me

I should be feeling like a celebrity right now that you’ve decided to read about me, I must confess I’m humbled. 😇

I know I need to put up a good article about me on this page but I’m sorry if the points seem scattered because I’m just going to write the way things pop into my head.

I am Trevor David Coker, a Nigerian guy in his early 20s studying Software Development at the National Institute of Information Technology. Truth is, I started studying the science of computers criminal way before college.🙃

David is an easy going (my mum might disagree)😅, funny guy that makes friends (and also cuts them) easily. Can’t really say about my being funny though, but I feel like the world’s best comedian when talking to friends, maybe they’re giving me false impressions.

Don’t know whether to use “David” now or use “I” but either way, this boy writing this loves tech.😍💜 I mean LOVES to the letter and likes to be updated even to the minute about the happenings in the tech world. 💜

Professionally, David is known as diligent, proactive, and easily adaptable to new environments🎯 (at least my employers so far agree).

Next vision is learning C# and C, i hope i remember to update this page when I’m done.
